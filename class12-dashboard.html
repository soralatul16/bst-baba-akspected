AKSpected Strategy

Unit 6-8 (Staffing + Directing + Controlling) = 20 marks — the single highest-weighted unit. Combined with Unit 9-10 (Financial chapters) = 15 marks and Unit 11-12 (Marketing + Consumer) = 15 marks, these 7 chapters carry 50 out of 80 marks (62.5%). Focus here first.
