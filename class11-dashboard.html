Class 11 — Build Your Base

📖 11 Chapters 🏗️ Foundation Year 📌 Strong base = less panic in Class 12
Why Class 11 Matters

Class 12 BM builds directly on Class 11 concepts. Topics like Forms of Business Organisation, Sources of Finance, and Business Services appear again in Class 12 in deeper forms. Learn them well now and you'll have half the Class 12 syllabus already understood.
