About Aakassh Soral

Aakassh Soral (AKS) is a qualified educator with multiple postgraduate degrees — MBA, M.Com in Business Administration, M.Com in EAFM, B.Ed, and LL.B. He specializes in CBSE Business Studies for Class 11 and 12.

With years of experience teaching Business Studies, AKS has developed his own approach to board exam preparation — the AKSpected Method. His notes combine NCERT theory with real case studies, previous year questions, and practice sets designed to match the exact CBSE board pattern.

BSt Baba — AKSpected is his platform to make quality Business Studies preparation accessible to every student, free of cost. The website includes chapter-wise notes, 2,850+ MCQs across 95 topics, flashcards, PYQ banks, mock tests, and 9 curated practice papers.

AKS is also available for private tutoring — both online and offline — for students who want focused, one-on-one guidance for their board exams.
